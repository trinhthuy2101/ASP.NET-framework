    What's in her song?

    "Wildest Dreams" is about a relationship doomed to end. However, Taylor is not yet ready to let go.
    Not only is he attractive, but he's masculine and assertive--he's her perfect man.
    The lyrics explain that while they are still together now, Swift foresees the end of the
    relationship and hopes that this man won't completely forget her.
